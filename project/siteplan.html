About Me

My name is Fernando Galvez and I am 22 years old, born in Culiacan, Sinaloa, Mexico. My passion for programming began 2 years ago when I had the opportunity to take an intensive 6-month full-stack development course. During that time, I realized that this was the career I wanted to pursue.

I then enrolled in an online Software Development degree program at Brigham Young University - Idaho. There, I learned many essential basics. Six months after completing my intensive course, I secured a job as a Junior Developer. I quickly realized the importance of improving my English to be more competitive in my career, so I moved to the United States to master the language and position myself for opportunities in U.S. software companies.

Currently, I work at a software company called CRMB as an Application Specialist. In this role, I train clients on our software applications, ensuring they understand how to utilize our products effectively. My goal is to transition into a more development-focused role in the future.
