They seek answers to questions about the origin of this remarkable transformation â€“ from a novice to a proficient programmer. About Me

My name is Fernando Galvez and I am 22 years old, born in Culiacan, Sinaloa, Mexico. My love for programming began a year ago when I received an offer to take a 6-month intensive course to be a full-stack developer. While taking that course I knew that this was the career I wanted to study. Then enter online college with a Software Development degree at Brigham Young University - Idaho. There I began to learn many super important basics, 6 months after finishing my intensive programming course I got a job as a Junior Developer and I realized that I needed to improve my English to be more competitive in my career, so I moved to the United States to master the English language and then be able to work for a software company in the United States. Today I am working in a software company called CRMB, my role in this company is to be an application specialist, knowing how it works to be able to train our clients. I hope later to be more involved in the development side.
